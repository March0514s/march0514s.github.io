About

I'm March!, a Frontend web developer from São Paulo, Brazil. After a long adventure through the dark realms of accountacy, taxing and bureaucracy I've decided to change my career so that I could put my programming skills to use and scratch that creative itch that was somehow left unatended for a huge period of my life. I'm currently focusing my efforts into making the web a better and more beautiful place through the usage of technologies like HTML5, CSS3, Javascript and React. If you're somehow interested in my work, projects or simply have similar ideas and would like to discuss them, please drop me a line.
